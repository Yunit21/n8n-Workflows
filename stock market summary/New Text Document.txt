This workflow automatically checks the stock market on a schedule.

It first checks if the US stock market is open.

If the market is closed, it does nothing.

If the market is open:

It looks at a fixed list of stocks.

It fetches recent daily price data for those stocks.

It calculates two basic indicators (RSI and MACD).

Based on simple rules, each stock is marked as Buy, Sell, or Hold.

The results are formatted and sent as a message on Telegram.
